Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees, and line two cookie sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Beat together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until it appears creamy and smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, and the vanilla until fluffy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt, then gradually add the dry mixture to the butter mixture. Stir in the oats and raisins.
  4. Drop teaspoonfuls of batter onto the prepared cookie sheets.
  5. Bake for about 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Let the cookies cool completely.
